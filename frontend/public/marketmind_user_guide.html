F1 — AI Price Target with Confidence Band

WHERE Stock DeepDive → Chat panel (right side of chart)
HOW AI responds with analysis AND auto-plots a shaded confidence band on the candlestick chart. No extra clicks. Band shows Confidence Low (green) and Confidence High (red) as separate series. Look for "AI Target" in the chart legend.
  1. Open any stock page — e.g. navigate to /stock/RELIANCE
  2. Find the chat panel on the right side of the chart
  3. Click "New Chat" if no session is active
  4. Type any message asking for a price target or chart analysis
  5. AI replies with analysis AND automatically plots a shaded band on the chart
  6. The target band shows Confidence Low (green) and Confidence High (red) price levels
  7. Ask follow-up questions — the band updates with each new target suggestion

Chips already in the UI:

Is this a good entry right now? Identify key support and resistance Explain the recent price move What's the risk/reward for next 3 months? Set an alert at key support level Set a stop loss alert for me Alert me at the breakout level

Anti-Gravity prompts — paste into chat:

Basic price target
Based on the current chart and indicators, what is your price target for this stock over the next 3 months? Give me a confidence range.
Entry + upside target
What is a realistic buy target and a 6-month upside target for this stock? Plot the range on the chart.
Bull vs bear target
Give me a bull-case target and a bear-case support level based on the technicals. I want to see both on the chart.
Institutional target
What would an institutional analyst set as a 12-month price target here? Give me a base case, bull case, and stop-loss level.

F2 — Pattern Recognition Auto-Detect

WHERE Stock DeepDive → Coloured badges near the top of the chart area
HOW Loads silently on every page open — no action needed. Detects patterns with ≥60% confidence. Click any badge to open the Pattern Detail Modal showing target price, stop-loss, and confidence. Press "Ask AI to Analyse This Pattern" to pre-load the chat.
  1. Open any stock page — patterns load automatically, no action required
  2. Look for coloured badges near the top of the chart (e.g. "Cup & Handle 82%")
  3. Click any badge to open the Pattern Detail Modal
  4. Modal shows: pattern description, target price, stop-loss level, confidence score
  5. Press "Ask AI to Analyse This Pattern" — opens chat with full pattern context pre-loaded
  6. Patterns above 60% confidence are shown; below 60% are silently discarded

Patterns detected:

  • Bullish: Cup & Handle, Inverse Head & Shoulders, Double Bottom, Ascending Triangle, Bull Flag, Falling Wedge, Morning Star, Golden Cross
  • Bearish: Head & Shoulders, Double Top, Descending Triangle, Bear Flag, Rising Wedge, Evening Star, Death Cross, Rounding Top
  • Neutral: Symmetrical Triangle, Rectangle, Doji Cluster, Inside Bar

Auto-injected prompt (already wired — fires when pressing "Ask AI" in the modal):

Explain the [PATTERN NAME] pattern on this chart and tell me if I should act on it.

Anti-Gravity Prompts:

Measured move + stop-loss
What is the measured move target for this pattern and where should I place my stop-loss to keep risk under 5%?
Pattern + fundamentals check
The chart shows a [PATTERN NAME] forming. Do the fundamentals support this setup or is there a reason to be cautious?
Historical reliability
How historically reliable is this pattern on Indian mid-cap stocks? What is the typical success rate and time to target?
Failure / invalidation scenario
What would invalidate this pattern? At what price level does this setup fail and what should I do if it breaks down?

F3 — Move Explanation Cache

WHERE Portfolio → Performance Center → Winners or Losers table → ⚡ button on any row
HOW Modal shows headline, explanation, catalyst chips, sentiment pill, and "AI Recommendation". Cached 4 hours. If the stock moves >2% the cache auto-invalidates. "Open Full Chart" button navigates to the stock's DeepDive page.
  1. Navigate to Portfolio → Performance Center
  2. Select a time period — week, month, year, or YTD
  3. Find the Winners or Losers table
  4. Click the ⚡ button on any row to trigger the AI explanation
  5. Modal opens with: headline, explanation, catalyst chips, sentiment, AI recommendation
  6. Click any stock name in the modal to navigate to its DeepDive page
  7. Results cached 4 hours — re-clicking shows instantly

Existing chips in the modal's chat panel:

F4 — AI-Generated Entry/Exit Alerts

WHERE Stock DeepDive → Chat panel
HOW Type a natural-language alert request. The chat engine intercepts alert keywords (set an alert, set alert, alert me, notify me, set a stop, alert at) and routes to the alerts API. AI extracts the exact price from the chart context (SMA, BB, 52w high/low, support/resistance) and confirms in its reply. Alerts appear in the "Active Alerts" list below the chips. Checked end-of-day against latest close.
  1. Open any stock page and go to the chat panel
  2. Click "New Chat" if no session is active
  3. Type a natural language alert request (see prompts below)
  4. AI extracts the exact price level from the chart context and confirms in its reply
  5. Alert appears in the "Active Alerts" section below the chat chips
  6. A bell badge on the page header shows how many active alerts exist
  7. Alerts are checked end-of-day against the latest closing price
  8. To delete: click the × button next to any alert in the Active Alerts list

Alert types supported: ENTRY, EXIT, SUPPORT, RESISTANCE — direction ABOVE or BELOW

Existing chips in the UI:

Set an alert at key support level Set a stop loss alert for me Alert me at the breakout level

Anti-Gravity Prompts:

Support alert
Set an alert if this stock falls to the key support level below the current price.
52-week high breakout
Alert me if the stock breaks above the 52-week high. I want to buy on breakout confirmation.
SMA stop-loss
Set a stop-loss alert just below the 50-day SMA. Notify me if the close crosses below that level.
Bollinger Band dual alert
Set two alerts: one if price touches the upper Bollinger Band and one if it touches the lower band.
Golden cross alert
Notify me when the 20-day SMA crosses above the 50-day SMA on this stock.

F5 — Expert Skill Chat

WHERE Stock DeepDive → Chat header → click the 🎭 icon → select a persona
HOW Switching persona starts a fresh chat session automatically. Each persona has its own quick chips. Messages route to /api/stock/{symbol}/skill_chat instead of the default /chart_chat. The active persona name and icon show in the chat header.
  1. Open any stock page and find the 🎭 icon in the chat panel header
  2. Click it to open the Expert Skill Picker dropdown
  3. Select a persona — the chat header updates with the expert's name and icon
  4. Skill-specific quick chips appear below the chat
  5. Every message is now analysed through that expert's lens
  6. Switching persona starts a fresh session — previous messages are not carried over
  7. To return to the default AI, select "Default" from the picker

F7 — Alpha Discovery / ML Discovery

WHERE AI Intelligence → Alpha Discovery
HOW Scans the entire market index to surface fundamental anomalies, technical breakouts, and AI-predicted momentum shifts. Used to discover actionable trade setups before they hit the mainstream news.
  1. Navigate to AI Intelligence → Alpha Discovery from the sidebar

F8 — The Oracle AI

WHERE AI Intelligence → The Oracle
HOW A centralized conversational interface for broad market thesis testing, multi-stock comparisons, and macro-economic inquiries not limited to a single stock ticker. It acts as your macro hedge-fund analyst.
  1. Navigate to AI Intelligence → The Oracle
  2. Ask broad questions e.g., "How does the RBI rate cut affect PSU banks vs Private banks?"
  3. Ask to compare multiple stocks e.g., "Compare the valuation of HDFC Bank and ICICI Bank"
  4. The Oracle maintains conversation context just like standard ChatGPT, but injected with live MarketMind data.

F9 — War Room (Institutional Research)

WHERE AI Intelligence → War Room
HOW A command center for deep-dive fundamental tearing apart of a company. Extracts quantitative scores (intel scores) from complex filings, supply chain dependencies, and competitor matrices.
  1. Navigate to AI Intelligence → War Room
  2. Search for a stock ticker to initiate a War Room snapshot
  3. Review the Institutional Intel Score and deep fundamental breakdown
  4. Switch between competitor matrix views and historical snapshots

F10 — Portfolio Optimizer (MVO/HRP/CVAR)

WHERE AI Intelligence → Portfolio Opt
HOW Uses advanced financial mathematics (Mean-Variance Optimization, Hierarchical Risk Parity, Conditional Value at Risk) to suggest optimal capital allocation across your watchlists or existing portfolio to maximize the Sharpe ratio.
  1. Navigate to AI Intelligence → Portfolio Opt
  2. Select the assets you want to invest in (or import your current portfolio)
  3. Choose your optimization strategy (e.g. Max Sharpe or Min Volatility)
  4. Click "Run Optimization" to generate the weight allocations
  5. Review the efficient frontier chart and the recommended target weights

F11 — Alpha Heatmap

WHERE Market → Alpha Heatmap
HOW A dynamic, visually striking tree-map representation of your portfolio's or the market's performance, instantly highlighting concentration risks, sector weighting, and outsized movers.
  1. Navigate to Market → Alpha Heatmap
  2. View the full tree-map grid (green for gainers, red for losers)
  3. Larger blocks indicate a higher weight or market cap vs smaller peers
  4. Hover over any block to see detailed exact percentage changes

F12 — Portfolio Performance Center

WHERE Market → Performance Center
HOW The central hub for tracking portfolio health. Features tables for Top Winners/Losers (which link directly to the F3 Move Explanation modal) and overall metric summaries.
  1. Navigate to Market → Performance Center
  2. Toggle between viewing Week, Month, Year, and YTD performance
  3. Review your biggest winners and losers
  4. Click the ⚡ icon next to any stock to trigger an AI Move Explanation (F3)

F13 — Benchmark Dashboard

WHERE Market → Performance (Benchmark Dashboard)
HOW Tracks long-term portfolio returns specifically against the Nifty 50. Houses the F6 Yearly Risk Explainer and tracks alpha generation over time.
  1. Navigate to Market → Performance (from the sidebar)
  2. Review the historical equity curve comparing your portfolio to the Nifty
  3. View the calendar-year breakdowns
  4. Click the "Why this year?" button to launch the Yearly Risk Explainer (F6)
