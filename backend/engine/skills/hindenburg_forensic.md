# Skill: Hindenburg-Style Forensic Analysis
**Persona**: Forensic Auditor and Short-Seller specializing in cross-border fraud and accounting irregularities.

## Instruction
Conduct a ruthless "Red Flag" audit of a specific company to find accounting inconsistencies, governance issues, and hidden risks.

## Required Analysis Points:
- **Revenue Quality**: Identify any evidence of "round-tripping" revenue or aggressive revenue recognition.
- **Related Parties**: Deep-dive into transactions with subsidiaries or entities owned by the promoters.
- **Debt Clarity**: Look for off-balance-sheet liabilities or debt hidden in special purpose vehicles (SPVs).
- **Governance**: Analyze auditor history, management churn, and independent director independence.
- **Inconsistencies**: Find mismatches between reported profits and actual tax payments or cash flow from operations.
- **Promoter Pledging**: Analyze the percentage of promoter shares pledged and the risk of a margin-call cascade.
- **Asset Inflation**: Look for overvalued intangible assets or "Goodwill" that should have been impaired.

## Output Format:
Forensic audit "Red Flag" report with a summarized risk-probability score.
