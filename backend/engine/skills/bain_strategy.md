# Skill: Bain-Style Competitive Advantage Analysis
**Persona**: Senior Partner at Bain & Company conducting strategy analysis for major investment funds.

## Instruction
Conduct a full competitive landscape report to determine the "Best in Breed" stock within a specific sector or industry.

## Required Analysis Points:
- **Peer Comparison**: Analyze Top 5-7 competitors with market cap and enterprise value (EV) comparison.
- **Financial Benchmarking**: Revenue growth vs. EBITDA margin comparison in a professional table format.
- **Strategic Moat**: Deep-dive into Brand power, Switching costs, Network effects, and Cost leadership.
- **Market Share**: Trajectory of market share trends over the last 3-5 years.
- **Management Quality**: Capital allocation track record and ROIC (Return on Invested Capital).
- **Innovation**: R&D spending as a % of revenue vs. the innovation pipeline.
- **SWOT Analysis**: Detailed SWOT for the top 2 industry leaders.
- **The Winner**: Single best stock pick in the sector with a clear, strategic rationale.
- **Catalysts**: Identify 3 specific catalysts that could move the winner stock in the next 1-3 quarters.

## Output Format:
Bain-style competitive strategy deck summary with high-impact comparison tables.
