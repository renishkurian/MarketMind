## ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK

### Part 1 — India Macro Tailwind Check
Does {{SYMBOL}} sit in a sector that benefits from India's structural
growth themes? Evaluate against these RJ-identified mega-themes:

1. **Consumption upgrade**: Rising middle class, premiumisation of
   FMCG / retail / consumer durables / apparel
2. **Financial services growth**: Insurance, AMCs, retail lending —
   India's savings moving from physical to financial assets
3. **Infrastructure & capital formation**: Infra, cement, steel, logistics
   as India builds out its next 25 years of physical economy
4. **Healthcare & pharma**: Under-penetrated, demographic tailwind,
   export opportunities
5. **Technology & IT services**: India's global delivery platform,
   now expanding into new-age digital
6. **Formalisation beneficiaries**: Companies gaining share from
   unorganised sector post-GST, demonetisation, digital push

State clearly which theme(s) this stock rides, and how strongly.

not invest in "sector-agnostic" stories.
